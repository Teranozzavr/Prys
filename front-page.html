A single change in pricing and packaging can lead to double-digit ARR growth.

Yet, most low-touch SaaS businesses rarely change pricing. Almost all who do change pricing and packages, do it without testing and getting clean data. They make the switch, and hope for the best.

Prys lets you optimize your pricing and packaging in the way you optimize other aspects of your business.

A 1-2 day integration with Prys's API gives you the flexibility to create packages, set prices, features and usage counters, without the need for a developer. You can run concurrent A/B tests on different packages, see relative performance, and make data-driven decisions.
